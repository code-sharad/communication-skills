**Total Duration**: 16 Weeks
**Philosophy**: Output > Input. If you didn't do the exercise, you didn't learn the skill.

---

## üß± Month 1: The Foundation (Precision & Vocabulary)
*Goal: Stop sounding vague. Start sounding sharp.*

- **Week 1: The Verb Upgrade**
    - Focus: Replacing "do/make/get" with precise action words.
    - **Output**: 5 rewritten emails/texts.
- **Week 2: The Adjective Detox**
    - Focus: Banning "good/bad/very". Emotional Vocabulary.
    - **Output**: Daily "Mood Log" using precise emotional words.
- **Week 3: The Fluff Remover (Conciseness)**
    - Focus: Eliminating "basically, actually, like". SVO Structure.
    - **Output**: 3 recordings (Before vs. After editing).
- **Week 4: The 3 Modes Drill**
    - Focus: Switching between Professional, Casual, Emotional.
    - **Output**: "Translation" exercises (Same message, 3 ways).

---

## üèóÔ∏è Month 2: The Structure (Logic & Clarity)
*Goal: Stop rambling. Start making points.*

- **Week 5: PREP Mastery (Everyday Logic)**
    - Focus: Point, Reason, Example, Point.
    - **Output**: Use PREP in 1 daily standup or meeting.
- **Week 6: The Executive Summary**
    - Focus: Explaining complex things simply (Pyramid Principle).
    - **Output**: Summarize a news article in 3 sentences.
- **Week 7: Persuasion (Crisis ‚Üí Hope)**
    - Focus: Selling ideas and convincing people.
    - **Output**: Write a "pitch" for a personal goal/project.
- **Week 8: Storytelling (The Golden Boat)**
    - Focus: Narrating your journey/experiences.
    - **Output**: Record your "Tell me about yourself" story (Final Version).

---

## üéôÔ∏è Month 3: The Delivery (Voice & Presence)
*Goal: It's not what you say, it's how you say it.*

- **Week 9: Voice Modulation**
    - Focus: Pace, Pause, Tone, Energy.
    - **Output**: Read a poem/speech with 3 different emotions.
- **Week 10: Body Language (Video Week)**
    - Focus: Eye contact, hand gestures, posture.
    - **Output**: Video record a presentation. Analyze hands/eyes.
- **Week 11: Impromptu Speaking**
    - Focus: Thinking on your feet.
    - **Output**: 30-second random topic drills (daily).
- **Week 12: Active Listening & Questioning**
    - Focus: Making others feel heard.
    - **Output**: Log of "Questions Asked" vs "Statements Made".

---

## üöÄ Month 4: Mastery (Real World Application)
*Goal: perform under pressure.*

- **Week 13: Difficult Conversations**
    - Focus: Saying "No", giving bad news, setting boundaries.
    - **Output**: Roleplay script for a conflict.
- **Week 14: Multilingual Fluidity**
    - Focus: Switching English/Hindi/Marathi without losing confidence.
    - **Output**: Explain the same concept in all 3 languages.
- **Week 15: Public Speaking Simulation**
    - Focus: The 5-minute talk.
    - **Output**: Prepare and record a full 5-minute talk.
- **Week 16: The Final Capstone**
    - Focus: Reviewing Month 1 vs Month 4 recordings.
    - **Output**: The "Before & After" Case Study.

---

## üîÑ The Weekly Loop (How to Practice)

1.  **Monday**: **Study** the concept (15 mins).
2.  **Tuesday - Thursday**: **Apply** in real life (Work/Home).
3.  **Friday**: **Record** a practice session.
4.  **Saturday**: **Review** and Critique your recording.
5.  **Sunday**: **Rest** & Reset.
