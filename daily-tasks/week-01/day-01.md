# Day 1: The Verb Audit
**Time Required**: 15 Minutes
**Type**: Output

---

## 1. The Concept (2 mins)
Weak verbs kill your authority. Strong verbs build it.
*   **Weak**: "I *did* the report."
*   **Strong**: "I **finalized** the report."

**Rule for Today**: Stop using "do", "make", "get", "say".
