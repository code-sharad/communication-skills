# Day 4: SVO Sentence Structure
**Time Required**: 15 Minutes
**Type**: Construction

---

## 1. The Concept (2 mins)
English is clearest in **Subject + Verb + Object** order.
*   **Confusing**: "With regards to the project, the completion was done by me."
*   **Clear**: "I completed the project."

**Rule for Today**: Who did what?
