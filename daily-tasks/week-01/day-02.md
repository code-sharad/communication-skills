# Day 2: The Adjective Swap
**Time Required**: 15 Minutes
**Type**: Awareness

---

## 1. The Concept (2 mins)
"Good", "Bad", "Nice", and "Fine" are lazy words. They mean nothing.
*   **Lazy**: "The meeting was good."
*   **Precise**: "The meeting was **productive**."

**Rule for Today**: You are banned from saying "Good" or "Bad".
