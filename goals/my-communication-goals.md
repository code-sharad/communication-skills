# My Communication Goals

**Date Started**: February 5, 2026

---

## Primary Goal
**Express thoughts in an articulated manner using the right words in the right sequence**

---

## Specific Goals

### Short-term Goals (0-4 weeks)
- [ ] Learn and practice the PREP framework daily
- [ ] Eliminate 3 common filler words from my speech
- [ ] Complete Week 1-4 exercises consistently
- [ ] Record and analyze 1 conversation per week
- [ ] Build vocabulary list of 50 precise words in each language

### Mid-term Goals (1-3 months)
- [ ] Speak in structured manner in professional meetings
- [ ] Explain technical concepts clearly to non-technical people
- [ ] Have difficult conversations without confusion
- [ ] Improve storytelling in personal contexts
- [ ] Reduce nervousness when speaking in groups

### Long-term Goals (3-6 months)
- [ ] Become confident public speaker
- [ ] Switch between English/Marathi/Hindi fluently while maintaining clarity
- [ ] Help others improve their communication
- [ ] Deliver presentations without over-preparation
- [ ] Develop my unique communication style

---

## Areas to Improve

### Professional Communication
- Explaining ideas in meetings
- Writing clear emails
- Presenting work to team/clients
- Networking conversations

### Personal Communication
- Expressing feelings clearly
- Having meaningful conversations
- Setting boundaries politely
- Storytelling with friends/family

### Structural Issues
- Organizing thoughts before speaking
- Maintaining logical flow
- Choosing precise words
- Completing thoughts without trailing off

---

## Languages Focus
- **English**: Professional and academic contexts
- **Marathi**: Family and cultural contexts
- **Hindi**: Mixed social and professional contexts

---

## Weekly Check-in Questions
1. Did I practice the PREP framework this week?
2. What communication challenge did I face?
3. What improved compared to last week?
4. What specific skill do I need to focus on next?
5. Did I complete all daily practices?

---

## Success Indicators
- People understand me on the first try
- I don't need to repeat or rephrase constantly
- I feel confident before speaking
- Conversations flow naturally
- I can explain complex ideas simply
- I choose words quickly and accurately
